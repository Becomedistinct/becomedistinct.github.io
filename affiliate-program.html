Common Questions

Everything you need to know before applying.

Is there any cost to join?
No. Completely free. We earn when the campaigns you refer become paying clients. There are no fees, minimums, or quotas ‚Äî ever.
How does sub-partner earning work exactly?
When you recruit someone into the program with your referral link, they become a sub-partner. They earn 10% on their direct referrals ‚Äî same as you on yours. You earn an additional 5% on those same campaigns, from our side, not theirs. It goes one level deep.
Can I refer campaigns I'm already consulting for?
Yes. If you already work with a candidate and you bring them to us, you can earn the referral commission. We'd encourage transparency with your client, but there's no restriction.
How does tracking work for verbal introductions?
Your unique link tracks online referrals automatically. For verbal introductions, have the candidate mention your name when they book their strategy call ‚Äî we'll log it manually. When in doubt, reach out to us directly.
What if a candidate doesn't sign right away?
Referral tracking has a 90-day window. If someone uses your link today and signs up 60 days from now, you still get full credit. Campaign decisions take time ‚Äî especially early in an election cycle.
Do commissions end when the election ends?
Commissions continue as long as the client stays on any paid plan. Winners often maintain a digital presence. Party organizations are year-round clients. And some candidates run again.
Can I refer both Republicans and Democrats?
Absolutely. We work with candidates from both parties. The only restriction: once we take one candidate in a contested race, we won't take their opponent. That's our commitment to our clients ‚Äî not a limit on who you can refer.
Is there a cap on how much I can earn?
None. Refer as many campaigns as you know. Recruit as many partners as you want. Every active campaign and sub-partner contributes to your monthly income ‚Äî indefinitely, without a ceiling.
